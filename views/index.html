Scott Reynolds

I'm Scott Reynolds and I've played music in about...um... a lot of bands over the years.
The most notable entry in my resume is singer and 1/4 of the songwriting department for the L.A.
based post-punk-pop-jazz pioneers ALL. I left that band a "few" (a relative word) years ago
and to make records and tour with other kickass bands: Goodbye Harry (versions 1 and 2),
the Pavers, Bonesaw Romance, and Scott Reynolds and the Steaming Beast, to name a few.
Now-a-days I'm playing, recording, and touring pretty much on my own or maybe with an old friend or two.
So watch for me. I'll probably turn up at a venue near you!
